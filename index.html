My name is Rayane computer science graduate from Higher School of computer science Algiers.
The goal of this website is to get you the essentials to get started with the field of computer science.
  • In chapter 00, we will explore fundamentals concepts used to find solution in computer science, often refferred to as algorithms.Our focus in this chapter will be on the importance of the problem-solving mindset. We will discover that programming languages are actually tools to express and translate our thought processes into instructions that a computer can understand!
    This chapter will cover the concepts:
    1. General overview of an algorithm.
    2. Sequential flow : Understanding the basic execution of simple actions such as declaring variables and performing read/write and other actions and operations.
    3. Control flow : Exploring how decisions and branching (e.g., if statements) affect the execution path of a program.
    4. Loops : Introduction to iteration and how to repeat actions efficiently using loops (e.g., for and while loops).
    5. Functions : A way to modularize the thought process and reorganize the solution.
    6. Introduction to complex data structures : structs and arrays.
  • In chapter 01, we will explore the essentials of computer architecture and how does a computer works. The goal of this chapter is to give an insight of how is your computer understanding the program you are feeding him.
